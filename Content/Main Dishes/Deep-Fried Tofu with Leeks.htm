Deep-Fried Tofu with Leeks (Niranegi To Yaki Dofu)

Recipe courtesy of Japanese Vegetarian Cooking, 1996

Approx. 10oz. fresh tofu

Cornstarch

2 young smallish leeks

½ ounce fresh ginger root

4 tbsp Japanese soy sauce

1 tbsp mirin

1 tbsp sugar

1 cup water

Vegetable oil for deep frying

 

Wrap the tofu in a clean kitchen towel and leave for 30 minutes to drain off excess water. remove roots and tough outer leaves from the leeks. Cut ¾ of the way through into the white part. Wash well under running water. Finely slice. Remove any discolored parts from the ginger and cut into match sticks.

 

In a large saucepan, heat the oil to 350°F and deep-fry the tofu for about 3 minutes, or unitl crisp and golden brown on the outside. Drain on absorbent paper towels.

 

Heat about 1 tbsp oil in a frying pan. Add leeks and ginger and stir-fry for 2 - 3 minutes. Add soy sauce, mirin, sugar and water, stirring a little. Add fried tofu and simmer for 2 - 3 minutes, stirring. Serve immediately.

 

Serve over Jasmine Rice

 

 

 

Yields: 4 servings
