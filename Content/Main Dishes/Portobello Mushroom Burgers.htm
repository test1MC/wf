Portobello Mushroom Burgers

Recipe adapted from The South Beach Diet, Quick and Easy Cookbook

 

1 cup textured vegetable protein (TVP) (We used "Smart Ground")

4 ounces part-skim mozzarella cheese, shredded (1 cup)

1 large egg, lightly beaten

3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided

2 garlic cloves, minced

3 medium portobello mushrooms, stemmed and chopped

1 small onion, finely chopped

1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes (packed in oil), chopped

Salt and freshly ground black pepper

 

Combine TVP, cheese, egg, 3/4 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper in a mixing bowl; stir to mix well.

 

Heat 2 tablespoons of the oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant, 30 seconds. Add mushrooms and onion; sauté, stirring often, until vegetables are softened and liquid is evaporated, 8 minutes. Stir in tomatoes.

 

Add vegetable mixture to egg mixture; stir to mix well. Cover and let stand 5 minutes.

 

Shape vegetable mixture into 6 (3-inch) patties. Heat remaining oil in a clean skillet over medium heat. Cook patties, turning once with a wide spatula, until lightly browned, 3 minutes per side.

 

  • Add chopped jalapeno chilies to spice it up
  • Serve as a regular burger, over gingered greens or create Lettuce Bun with romaine leaves.
  • If you find there is a lot of liquid, while trying to evaporate it, drain it through a colander and you will have some nice mushroom stock to use freeze and use in a soup later.

 

Yields: 5-6 burgers

Nutrition Information:

230 calories

16 total fat (5 g sat)

11 g carbohydrate

14 g protein

4 g fiber

190 mg sodium
