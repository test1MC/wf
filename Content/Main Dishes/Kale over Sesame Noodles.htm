Kale over Sesame Noodles

Recipe courtesy of Michela, 2009

1 bunch of kale

1 tsp olive oil

2 - 3 clove garlic, minced

8 oz soba noodles (dry)

2 tbsp toasted sesame oil

2 tbsp low sodium soy sauce

1 tsp +/- red chili flakes

2 tbsp sesame seeds

¼ lemon

 

Wash and remove the stem from the kale. Coarsely chop the kale and add to boiling water to blanche it, until just tender. With a slotted spoon or strainer, scoop out the kale. Reserve the water, you will need it for cooking the soba noodles.

 

Meanwhile, heat olive oil over medium heat in a large skillet. Add garlic and red chili flakes and cook until garlic is soft, then add the sesame oil and sesame seeds. Cook for another minute, stirring so it doesn't burn. Add the drained greens to the skillet and toss to mix. Cook the soba noodles in the remaining kale water.  When the noodles are soft, drain the liquid and add everything to the pot or sauce pan (whichever is easiest). Add soy sauce and heat. Add lemon, salt, pepper, chili flakes or more sesame oil to taste.

 

Optional: Saute sliced portobello mushrooms in sesame oil and soy sauce. Add to the completed dish just before serving.
