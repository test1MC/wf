Chipotle Polenta Cakes

Recipe courtesy of  Frank, 2006

Oil

2+ cups of polenta or use the leftover polenta from Kale and Mushrooms with Polenta (page 1)

½ cup sweet corn (cut from the husk)

3 - 4 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce (minced)

2 tbsp cilantro (chopped)

1 egg

If needed, salt and pepper to taste.

 

Mix all ingredients together in a bowl (add the egg after the flavor is right).

 

In a hot non-stick skillet add a drop of oil and fry cakes until golden. Place cakes on a baking sheet in the oven until ready to serve.

 

Top cakes with Salsa Fresca (page 1) and serve with a salad.

 

 

 

Serves 4
