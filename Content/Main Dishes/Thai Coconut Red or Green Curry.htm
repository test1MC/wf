Thai Coconut Red or Green Curry

Recipe courtesy of  Thai Kitchen, 2004

½ - 1 tbsp red/green curry paste (Thai Kitchen)

1 can pure coconut milk (14 oz.)

½ cup fresh basil - chopped

2 tbsp brown sugar

½ - 1 vegetable bullion ( this is to replace the fish sauce. use ½ and add the other ½ if it needs the flavor)

⅓ cup vegetable broth

Fresh beans

1 red/green bell pepper (if making the red curry, use the green pepper and visa versa)

Salt to taste

 

1 block of cubed and pan fried tofu (fried in Sesame oil)

 

In a medium sauce pan, add the coconut milk and curry paste. Use a wisk to break up the curry paste and simmer for 5 minutes, stiring often.

Add the basil, vegetables, vegetable bullion, broth and brown sugar. Simmer for an additional 10 - 15 minutes.

Add tofu and serve

 

Serve over jasmine rice

 

 

 

Yields 4 Servings
