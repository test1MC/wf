Marinated Ahi

Recipe courtesy of Michela, 2011

3 tablespoons unseasoned rice vinegar

2 tablespoons finely grated peeled fresh ginger

2 tablespoons peanut oil

2 tablespoons toasted sesame oil

2 tablespoons soy sauce

2 tablespoons honey

1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro

1 tablespoon minced garlic

chili flakes to taste

 

Freshly ground white pepper

Two ahi steaks 1 1/4 -inch-thick

Canola oil

 

Whisk first 9 ingredients in medium bowl to blend; season to taste with ground white pepper. Transfer 3 tablespoons marinade to small bowl and reserve. Add tuna to remaining marinade in a zip-lock bag and coat ahi. Leave out at room temperature for 30 to 45 minutes flipping the bag occasionally.

 

Prepare barbecue (high heat) and apply oil to the rack. Grill ahi to desired doneness, about 3 to 4 minutes total flipping after 1.5 minutes. Transfer to platter. Drizzle reserved marinade over; sprinkle with chopped cilantro.

 

Additional chopped fresh cilantro for garnish
