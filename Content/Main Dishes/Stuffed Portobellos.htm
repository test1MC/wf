Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms

Recipe adapted from The South Beach Diet by Michela, 2009

2 Large portobello mushroom caps, gills removed

2 tsp fresh garlic, chopped

1½ tsp dried oregano

1½ tsp dried basil

½ cup sun dried tomatoes (packed in olive oil), chopped

½ cup vine ripened tomatoes, chopped

1/4 tsp olive oil

black pepper to taste

2 oz part-skim mozzarella cheese, shredded

 

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. using a small spoon or knife, remove the gills from the portobello mushroom caps and remove the stem. In a bowl, mix all of the tomatoes, oregano, basil, garlic and olive oil and pepper. Place the mushroom caps in a baking sheet and place ½ of the tomato mixture in each. evenly top each mushroom with ½ of the cheese. Place in the oven for 15 minutes or until the cheese is slightly browned. Serve immediately.

 

Mix in some pine nuts in the tomato mixture (this will make this recipe not qualify for the south beach diet)

If you use sun-dried tomatoes that are not oil-packed add more olive oil.

 

Yields: 2 Mushrooms
