Dragon Potatoes & Yams

Recipe courtesy of Isabel's Cantina, 2007

1 large sweet potato

1 large yam

1 large potato (russet)

coarse salt

freshly ground black pepper

olive oil

 

Wash the potatoes and boil them with the skins on until tender but the middle is still firm. Drain and let cool. Peel the skin off of each potato and cut them into rounds about 1/2" to 3/4" thick.

Heat oil over medium-high heat. Add potatoes to the oil once hot. Cook for 2 - 3 minutes, until browned and crisp on each side. Salt and pepper each side immediately as they finish.

 

Serve with Chipotle Yogurt.
