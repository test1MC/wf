Napa Valley Tomatoes

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2004

Dill

3 - 4 vine ripened tomatoes

8 oz plain yogurt

1 lemon

1 clove of garlic

salt and pepper

 

Place yogurt, garlic, some dill in a food processor or blender. Blend until smooth. Add more dill lemon, salt and pepper to taste.

 

Cut tomatoes into wedges and align in a circular pattern around a plate.

Pour sauce evenly over the dish

 

Garnish the dish with tomato skin flowers and dill.

 

 

 

This recipe came form a night of cooking and wine on our 2004 trip to Napa Valley.

 

Yields: 4 - 6 servings
