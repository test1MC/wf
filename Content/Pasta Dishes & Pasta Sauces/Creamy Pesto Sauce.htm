Creamy Pesto Sauce

Recipe courtesy of Elodia Rigante's Italian Immigrant Cooking, 1995

½ cup chopped fresh basil

2 tbsp pine nuts

4 tbsp grated Romano cheese

4 large garlic cloves

1 cup olive oil

1 tsp coarsely chopped fresh parsley

½ tsp

salt

half & half (optional)

 

Regular:

Combine all of the ingredients in a blender and blend until very smooth. Serve over buttered pasta or gnocchi with freshly grated Romano cheese.

 

Creamy:

Heat the pesto in a small sauce pan until very hot but not sticking to the pan yet, and gradually stir in some half & half. Do Not Boil, the half & half will curdle. Serve over pasta or gnocchi with freshly grated Romano cheese.

 

Note: Sauce and oil will separate upon heating. You can blend it again just before serving (optional).

 

 

 

 

 

Yields enough for 1 lb of pasta (About 4 servings)
