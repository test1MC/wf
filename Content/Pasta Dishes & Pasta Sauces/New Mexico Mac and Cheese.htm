New Mexico Mac and Cheese

Recipe courtesy of Michela, 1999

one 6 oz box of Annie's Mild Mexican Shells & Cheddar (or regular Mac & Cheese)

2 tbsp butter

one 8 oz can of tomato sauce

1 jalapeno (minced)

8+ ounces of milk

 

Prepare the noodles as directed. Drain and put them back in the pot with the butter.

Add the tomato sauce, milk, jalapeno and stir until heated.

Stir in the cheese mix from the box.

Salt & pepper to taste.

 

Add cooked and sliced Boca Bratwurst or Italian sausages after the jalapeno.

 

 

 

This recipe is hardly a 'gourmet' meal and has changed many times over the years, but continues to be one of our comfort meals.

 

Yields: 2 - 3 servings as a meal
