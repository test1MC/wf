Leek and Brussels Sprout Pasta

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2004

Pasta (linguini is a good choice)

½ shallot, minced

8 Brussels sprouts

½ large leek (½ of green and ½ of white), sliced

butter

heavy whipping cream

2 - 3 garlic cloves, minced

3 - 4 sun dried tomatoes (packaged in olive oil), chopped

5+ kalamata olives (optional), sliced

 

Boil water for pasta. Add brussels sprouts and boild for about 5 minutes, until al dente. Remove from water, drain and quarter, set aside. Add pasts to water and cook.

 

While the brussels sprouts and pasta is cooking, heat butter in a large frying pan. Slowly cook garlic, shallots for about 5 minutes. Add leek and cook for another 5 minutes. Add cream and chopped tomatoes (olives too if you are using them) and cook for a few minutes, until hot. Add brussels sprouts quarters.

 

Toss with pasta and serve immediately.

 

Serve with warm fresh bread

 

This recipe was created in Napa Valley in March 2004. We shared it with our good friends Gregg and Jamie when they came from San Francisco to join us. Works well with a cold white wine, we served it with a Riesling.

 

Yields: serves 2
