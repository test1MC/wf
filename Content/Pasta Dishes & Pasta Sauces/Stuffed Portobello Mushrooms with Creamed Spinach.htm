Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms with Creamed Spinach

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2004

2 lbs. spinach, chopped

1 pt. heavy whipping cream

1 stick butter

1 oz. garlic, minced

2 oz. parsley, chopped

salt and pepper

1 cup sun-dried tomatoes, sliced

8 portobello mushrooms

2 cups pine Nuts

dash or 2 of crushed red pepper (to taste)

 

In a skillet melt butter and add spinach. Saute until spinach starts to wilt. Add garlic, salt, pepper, crushed red pepper, and parsley. Add cream and bring to a boil then add pine nuts and sun-dried tomatoes. Lower heat and reduce until cream turns into a rich and thick sauce.

 

Place filing into portobello mushroom and bake in a pre-heated 350 degree oven for 10 - 15 minutes.

 

Serve over a bed of spaghetti.

 

A light white wine would go nicely with this dish, 2004 Dr. "L" Riesling

 

We originally got this recipe from a friend from New Mexico that we were re-aquatinted with in San Diego. She had us over for dinner and made this. We got part of the recipe from her, edited it and made it our own.

 

Serves 8 (4 ounce portions)
