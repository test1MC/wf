Garlic Cream Sauce

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2002

½ cup Roasted garlic, pureed, minced

4 cups heavy cream

Salt and cayenne pepper to taste

 

In a saucepan combine roasted garlic and heavy cream and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to a simmer and cook until liquid has reduced by half, about 20 minutes.

 

In a blender, puree sauce (use caution if hot!), and season with salt and pepper to taste.

 

Add rosemary or other herbs to the sauce while it reduces .

Add more roasted garlic when pureeing the sauce if desired.

Serve over Gnocchi or pasta

 

Yields: enough for 1.5 lbs of pasta
