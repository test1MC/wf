Sweet Rice

Recipe courtesy of Michela, 2001

1¾ cups milk

¼ cup sugar -- divided

½ cinnamon stick

¼ cup long-grain rice

1 egg yolk

¼ cup heavy cream

½ teaspoon vanilla extract

¼ cup golden raisins (optional)

 

Heat milk, 1/8 cup sugar and cinnamon in saucepan just to boil. Stir in rice and reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer 40 minutes. Whisk yolks, cream, remaining 1/8 cup sugar and vanilla in bowl. Stir into rice mixture.

 

Bring to boil; cook, stirring, 2 minutes. Remove cinnamon. Cool 30 minutes.

 

 

Yields: 2+ servings
