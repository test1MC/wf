Tofu Scrambler

Recipe courtesy of Michela, 2001

½ block soft tofu

1½ tbsp scallion, minced

1 large clove garlic , minced

1 tsp olive oil

¼ cup mushrooms, diced

¼ cup yellow squash or zucchini, quartered and sliced

1 green onion, chopped

1 tsp curry powder

pinch of turmeric

1 small vine ripened tomato (chopped)

1 tbsp parsley, chopped

salt and pepper to taste

 

Heat the oil in a pan. Add scallion, garlic, mushroom, squash, green onion and cook for 2 to 3 minutes. Crumble the tofu into the pan and add curry powder and turmeric; Stir and cook until vegetables are almost done and most of the liquid is gone. Salt and pepper to taste and mix in parsley. Top with chopped tomatoes and serve.

 

This is a good substitution for scrambled eggs...

Add a jalapeno, diced with the vegetables for a little spice.

This recipe is ok for the South Beach Diet

 

Yields: 2 servings
