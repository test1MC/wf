Belgian Waffles

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2009

2½ cups flour (sifted)

4 tbsp sugar, white (sifted)

2 tsp baking powder (sifted)

½ tsp salt

 

3 eggs (separated)

2 cups milk

½ vegetable oil

1 tsp vanilla extract

 

Sift all 'dry' ingredients together and stir lightly to mix.

In a separate bowl beat together egg yolks and all of the other 'wet' ingredients, except the egg whites. Sift in the 'dry' ingredients and mix gently with a wooden spoon. Do not over-mix, having lumps is ok.

 

Whip egg whites until they are a thick foam and gently fold into the batter.

 

Lightly oil and pre-heat the waffle iron, cook until the waffles are the desired brownness.
