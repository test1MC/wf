Basil Pesto and Pasta Frittata

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2001

1 tbsp olive oil

6 large eggs

¼ cup of basil pesto

Pinch of salt

pepper

¾ cup Part-skim mozzarella cheese, shredded

6 oz. Dried pasta (Spaghetti or Linguine)

 

Put a few quarts of water on to boil for the pasta. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees. Rub the olive oil onto a heavy 10 inch skillet that can take the heat of the oven. Cook the pasta until al dente. Drain well. Distribute it evenly over the bottom of the skillet and allow to cool for 10 minutes.

 

Meanwhile, whisk the eggs with the pesto, salt, pepper, then stir in the cheese. Pour the egg mixture over the pasta, being sure that it's distributed evenly. Bake uncovered for 20 minutes or until golden on top.

 

Cut into wedges. Serve hot or at room temperature

 

Place asparagus across the top in a pattern before placing in the oven.

 

Yields: A 4 serving main dish, 6 serving side dish.
