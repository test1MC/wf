Artichoke and Basil Frittata

Recipe courtesy of Michela, 2009

1 bag of frozen artichoke hearts (Trader Joe's)

6 eggs

¼ cup fresh basil, chiffonade

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 tbsp shallot, minced

¼ cup non-fat milk

½ cup parmesan, mozzarella cheese, shredded

Salt and pepper to taste

 

Preheat the oven to 325 degrees.

Defrost artichoke hearts according to the package. In an oven-proof skillet, saute the garlic and shallots in olive oil (around 2 minutes).  Add artichokes and cook for another minute or so. In a large bowl beat the eggs with milk, add basil and salt and pepper.

Add the eggs to the skillet and place in the oven for about 20 - 30 minutes or until the eggs are set.

Cut using a wooden knife and serve while warm.

 

Top with a fresh salsa fresca or just some chopped up tomatoes.
