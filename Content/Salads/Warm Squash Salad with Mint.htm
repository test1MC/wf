Warm Squash Salad with Mint

Recipe courtesy of Unknown, 2008

1 tbsp olive oil

2 medium zucchini, trimmed and chopped into ½" pieces

2 small yellow crookneck squash, trimmed and cut into ½" pieces

coarse salt to taste

freshly ground black pepper to taste

1 (15.5 ounce) can of cannellini beans, rinsed, drained, and dried

¼ cup mint, loosely packed, chopped fresh

4 ounces goat cheese, crumbled

 

Add the olive oil to a large nonstick skillet and heat to medium high. Add all of the squash, season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring frequently for ten minutes or until the squash is lightly browned and tender (adjust heat if squash browns too quickly). Transfer to a large serving bowl.

 

Add the beans and most of the mint to the bowl. Toss gently. Just before serving, crumble the goat cheese over teh salad and finish with the rest of the mint.

 

Add some Quorn 'chicken' pieces to turn it into a meal.

 

 

 

Yields: 4 servings
