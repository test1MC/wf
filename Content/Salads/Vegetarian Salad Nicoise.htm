Niçoise Salad - Vegetarian

Recipe courtesy of South Beach Diet

2 large hard-boiled eggs, quartered

1/2 cup nicoise or kalamata olives

4 ounces haricots verts (French green beans)

2 1/2 cups mixed lettuces, chopped (red leaf, oak leaf, butter, Bibb, etc.)

4 ounces tempeh or firm tofu, diced

8 grape tomatoes, halved

1/2 red, orange or yellow bell pepper, cut in strips

2 teaspoons capers, rinsed

1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar

1 teaspoon Dijon mustard

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 tablespoon fresh herbs (any combination of fresh thyme, oregano, basil, parsley, chives, etc.)

kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste

 

Mix vinegar, oil, capers, mustard, and herbs in quart-sized zip-top bag. Shake vigorously. Add tempeh (or tofu) and let sit. Toss together lettuces, tomatoes, peppers, and olives, and place on plate. Remove tempeh or tofu and place around plate along with eggs. Dress with remaining dressing in baggie. Season to taste. Serve.

 

Try the herb salad mix, it is a nice variation. Also I marinated the tofu overnight which helped the flavors meld.

 

Yields: 2 servings
