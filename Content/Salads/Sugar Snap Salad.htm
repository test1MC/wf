Sugar Snap Salad

Recipe courtesy of The South Beach Diet

8 ounces sugar snap peas, (2 cups), trimmed

2 tablespoons white-wine vinegar

2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil

1⁄4 teaspoon salt

1/8 tsp freshly ground pepper

Freshly ground pepper to taste

 

Cook peas in lightly salted boiling water in a medium saucepan until tender-crisp, 2-3 minutes. Drain and rinse under cold water.

Wisk vinegar, oil, salt and pepper in a large bowl. Add peas, scallions and bell pepper, toss to coat. Serve within one hour.

Toss this with baby spinach and add some cut tomatoes and tofu to make more substantial.

 

Yields: 4 servings

Nutrition Information:

97 calories

8 total fat (3g sat, 19g mono)

0 mg cholesterol

7 g carbohydrate

2 g protein

2 g fiber

151 mg sodium
