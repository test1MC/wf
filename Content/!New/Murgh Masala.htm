Murgh Masala

Recipe courtesy of  Michela, 2005

2 lbs tofu (extra firm)

peanut oil (any oil good for pan frying will work)

1½ cup onion - finely chopped

2 tbsp ginger - finely chopped

1½ tbsp garlic - minced

1 tbsp paprika

1 - 1½ tsp ground red pepper

1½ lb vine ripened tomoatoes - pureed

1 cup vegetable broth

2 tsp garam masala (or ground cumin)

½ cup cilantro - chopped 

Salt to taste

 

Saute tofu in the peanut oil until golden, drain and set a side.

 

Saute the onion, ginger and garlic until the onion starts to brown. Add paprika, red pepper, tomatoes and salt to taste. Bring to a boil. Stir in the broth and return to a boil. Cover and let simmer for 30 minutes until it's reduce close to the thickness of a spaghetti sauce (remove lid and simmer if needed).

 

Stir in garam masala/cumin, half of the cilantro and add the tofu. Once hot serve.

 

Serve over jasmine rice

 

Yields 4 Servings
