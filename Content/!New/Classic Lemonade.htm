Classic Lemonade

Recipe courtesy of Unknown, 2004

1 cup sugar

5 cups water

Rind from 2 lemons (scrubbed and cut into pieces)

1 cup fresh lemon juice

 

Mix sugar, 1 cup of water and lemon rinds in a small saucepan. Heat to t a boil and simmer until the sugar dissolves. 5 – 6 minutes.  Cool and strain.  Mix the sugar syrup, remaining 4 cups of water and lemon juice.

 

Pour over ice.  

If desired, add several drops of grenadine for pink lemonade.

Serve with sugar rimmed glass and slice of lemon.
