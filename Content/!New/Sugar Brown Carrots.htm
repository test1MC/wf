Sugar Brown Carrots

Recipe courtesy of Sophia Kvacek

16 small carrots or large sliced carrots (1-2 lbs.)

¼ cup butter or margarine

6 Tbs. Sugar

Salt to taste

 

Cook peeled carrots in boiling water until tender-crisp. Drain well.

Melt butter in large skillet or saucepan. Stir in sugar and cook, stirring constantly until mixture is a light

caramel color. Add carrots, continue to cook, shaking the skillet or saucepan to brown carrots evenly.

Sprinkle with salt during cooking.

 

 

 

 

 

Yields: 6 - 8 servings
