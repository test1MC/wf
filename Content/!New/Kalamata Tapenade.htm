Kalamata Tapenade 

Recipe courtesy of Eric Bearden, 2004

Kalamata Olives

Capers

Mustard

Roasted Red Peppers

Small amount of good quality Brown Mustard

A few garlic cloves

Juice of one lemon

Fresh black pepper to taste

Fresh thyme

Olive Oil

 

Combine all ingredients in a food processor and blend, adding the olive oil until the mixture forms a paste.  Better if refrigerated for several hours before serving, or even over night.
