MIke's Margies

Recipe courtesy of  Mike Stevens, June 2008

MIke invited us over one night very excited to share this margarita with us. After two of them we were pretty much out of it.

1½ oz Tequila

½ oz Triple Sec

½ oz Grand Marnier

1 oz 'top shelf' sweet and sour mix

1 oz lime juice

splash oj

beer to taste

 

Mix all ingredients and sever.

 

 

 

Yields 8 Servings
