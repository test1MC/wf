Hash Brown Casserole

Recipe courtesy of  Helen Zillmer, December 2007

Frozen shredded hash browns, thaw and pat dry between towels

1 stick of butter, melted

2 cups sour cream

2 cups grated cheese

chives

 

Mix all ingredients and place into a 13x9 baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 - 60 minutes until lightly browned on top.

 

Optional: Sprinkle with paprika

 

These are great as potato pancakes the next day. Just make them into flat cakes, fry in a lightly oiled pan until brown on both sides and hot in the middle. Keep warm in the oven until ready to serve.

 

Makes around 10 servings
