Mediterranean Greens

Recipe courtesy of  Trader Joe's, 2005

½ - 1 lb Southern Greens Blend (from trader Joe's) - mustard greens, turnip greens, spinach & collard greens

¼ + cup sun dried tomatoes (oil marinated) - sliced

4 cloves of garlic

Ground black pepper to taste

2 tbsp pine nuts

½ cup black olives - sliced

½ cup pimento stuffed green olives - sliced

½ cup pitted kalamata olives - sliced

 ¼ cup olive oil

2 cups Roma tomatoes - cut into strips

1 - 3 cups of vegetable broth

Thin pasta

 

Cook olives, sun dried tomatoes, garlic, oil and pepper in a large skillet on medium high for 6 - 8 minutes. Stir in the Roma tomatoes and cook for an additional 2 minutes. Add greens (about ½ and about 1½ cups of broth). Cook for 25 - 30 minutes adding broth and veggies as needed to get a nice balance of sauce and vegetables.

Stir in pine nuts and salt to taste.

 

 

 Spoon over pasta and garnish with parmesan cheese.
