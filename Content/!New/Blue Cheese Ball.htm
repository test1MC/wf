Blue Cheese Ball

Recipe courtesy of  Helen Zillmer, December 2007

8 oz. cream cheese

1 cube of butter

2 - 3 green onions, finely chopped

1 small package of crumbled blue cheese

6 black olives, chopped

 

Mix butter, cream cheese and blue cheese together by hand. Fold in olives and onions. Form into a ball, wrap in saran-wrap and chill.

 

Letting warm for 30 minutes before serving makes it softer and easier to spread.

Serve with various crackers.
