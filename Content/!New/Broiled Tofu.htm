Broiled Tofu

Recipe courtesy of , Unknown

½ lb tofu cut into ¼ to ½ cubes or ¼ inch thick slices or strips

3 tbsp olive oil

3 tbsp soy sauce

 

Place the tofu in a baking dish oil and soy sauce and mix it up so that the tofu is well covered. Marinate in it anywhere from ten minutes to a few hours.

 

Set oven to 400+ and cook until golden brown or Broil the tofu until brown, or even crispy (5 to 15 minutes), turning it every few minutes.

 

Add different spices, herbs, oils and sauces. Tastes great with a little sesame oil and garlic.

 

Crispy slices make a great tofu, lettuce and tomato sandwich.

 

Serves: 2
