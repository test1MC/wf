Chipotle Corn Soup

Recipe courtesy of  Frank & Michela, 2005

Soup:

Olive oil

1 large yellow onion - diced

4 cloves garlic - minced

2 tsp chipotle in adobo sauce (one pepper minced and 1 tsp of sauce)

3½ cups of fresh corn - cut from kernels, keep kernels

6 cups vegetable stock

1 cup heavy cream

Salt and pepper to taste

 

Sauce:

1 red bell pepper

½ cup heavy cream

 

Soup:

In a soup pot, saute onions in the oil on medium-high heat until translucent (3 - 4 minutes). Add the garlic and chipotle and saute another minute.

Add the corn and cook for another 5 minutes, stirring often.

Add vegetable broth, salt, pepper and corn cobs, bring to a boil and simmer uncovered for 30 minutes. Stir occasionally.

Remove corn cobs and blend the soup until smooth. Pour through a sieve and put it back into the pot.

 

Add cup of cream and heat on low for about 10 minutes being careful not to boil it.

 

Sauce:

Roast the red pepper, skin and seed it. Pace it in a blender/food processor with ½ cup of heavy cream . Blend until smooth.

 

Garnish soup with the Roasted Red Pepper Sauce and Cream.
