Potato Soufflé

Recipe courtesy of Niki, 2004

8 medium sized russet potatoes

Butter

Milk

Salt

Pepper

Cheeses of your choice (at least 3 different varieties are good- pecorino, parmesan, and white cheddar is a good blend)

 

Peel and boil the potatoes, then mash with butter and milk.  Add salt and pepper to taste.  Could add chives, sour cream, etc.  

Whip the potatoes getting as much air into them as possible.  Take half the mixture and place .  Layer  starting with the potatoes and cheeses in a casserole dish, top off with plenty of cheese.  

Bake at 375 degrees until potatoes rise and cheese has browned.
