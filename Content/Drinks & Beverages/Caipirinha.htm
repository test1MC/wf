Caipirinha

Recipe courtesy of Emeril Lagasse, 2002

1 medium-size lime, cut into one-half-inch chunks

4 to 5 teaspoons sugar (or to taste)

2 to 2½ ounces Cachaça (Brazilian tequila)

Ice cubes or crushed ice

 

Put the lime chunks and the sugar into a thick-walled rocks glass and crush together until the pulp and the juice of the limes are well macerated. Add cachaça and ice. (Multiply by number of drinkers for pitcher batches)

 

Multiply the ingredients and serve in a pitcher

 

 

 

Yields: 1 drink
