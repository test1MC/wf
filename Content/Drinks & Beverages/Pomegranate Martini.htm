Pomegranate Martini

Recipe courtesy of Michela & Frank, 2009

1 part Quality Vodka

2 parts POM or other 100% Pomegranate Juice

Squeeze of Lemon

 

Add everything to the shaker, add ice, shake and strain. Garnish with a slice of lemon or a Lemon twist.

 

Optional: You can lessen the amount of POM Juice and add that amount of Pama.
