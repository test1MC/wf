Frank's Margaritas

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2001

150ml Anejo tequila (Jose Cuervo)

150ml Gold tequila (Sauza)

50ml Grand Marnier

50ml Cointreau

50ml Triple Sec

600ml Lemonade

1-2 tsp sugar

Lime

Salt (Optional)

 

Mix alcohol together and add one tsp sugar, mix we'll. Taste and adjust flavor using alcohol and additional sugar.

 

Serve with either salt or sugar rimmed glass and squeeze of lime.

 

 

 

Yields: 4 servings
