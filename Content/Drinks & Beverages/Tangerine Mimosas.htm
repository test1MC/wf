Tangerine Mimosas

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2005

1½ cups freshly squeezed tangerine juice

¾ cup Cointreau

Champagne or sparkling wine

 

Combine the tangerine juice and cointreau in a pitcher or carafe.

Fill flute ½ full of the mix and finish filling the flute with champagne.

 

Garnish with Tangerine twists
