Mojitos

Recipe courtesy of Unknown, 2009

Drink:

2 cups Light Rum

1 cup mint syrup

1 cup lime juice

fresh mint leaves

 

Syrup:

1¼ cup mint leaves, rinsed

1 cup water

½ cup sugar

 

Drink:

Combine rum, syrup, lime juice together. Pour into a glass with ice and mint leaves/stalks.

 

Syrup:

Combine all ingredients in a sauce pan. Stir over heat until the sugar is dissolved and the mixture simmers. Remove from heat and cover. Let stand for 30 minutes then strain.

Syrup can be stored, covered in the refrigerator for up to a week.
