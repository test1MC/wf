Mulled Wine

Recipe courtesy of Michela (adapted from 'unknown'), 2010

1 bottle red wine

4-5 whole allspice

4-5 whole cloves

1-2 whole star anise

1/2-3/4 cup sugar to taste (1/3 cup)

Peel of large orange no white

1/2 cup dark rum

1 small stick of cinnamon

Combine all ingredients in a pot and warm to a gentle steam. Do not simmer or boil. Keep at temp for 10 min then remove from heat. Mix in rum. Serve warm with a squeeze of orange.

 

When peeling the orange and lemon, do not include too much of the white part of the zest.
