Sangria

Recipe courtesy of "Cook, Eat, Cha, Cha, Cha", 2001

1 orange, cut into ⅛ inch slices

1 lemon

1 cup sugar

1 bottle dry white wine (Chardonnay)

1 bottle dry red wine (Cabernet Sauvignon)

Sliced peaches, pears, grapes, strawberries, or other seasonal fruit

Grand Marnier to taste

 

Combine the orange, lemon, sugar and wines in a large glass bowl and stir for several minutes. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 to 6 hours, or overnight. Add the other fruits and spirits, if you like.

 

Serve over ice or, just before serving, place some ice cubes in the punch bowl.

 

 

 

Yields: 8 one cup servings
