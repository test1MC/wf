Avgolemono Soup

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2009

2 tbsp olive oil

2 medium leeks, white parts finely chopped (around 4 cups)

1 medium sweet onion, finely chopped (around 1 cup)

2 carrots, peeled and diced (around 3/4 to 1 cup)

6+ cups of non-chicken broth

1/2 cup orzo pasta, dried

2 large eggs (optional)

3 tbsp fresh lemon juice

¼ cup fresh parsley, chopped

1 tbsp fresh mint, chopped

1 tbsp fresh oregano, chopped

12 oz Quorn chicken tenders, cooked and chopped

Salt and pepper, a good amount of pepper

Lemon wedge, peels

 

Heat the oil in a soup pot over medium heat. Add leeks, onion carrot and a pinch of salt; cover and cook, stirring often until soft (5 - 7 minutes). Stir in broth. season with a little salt and pepper. heat until it begins to simmer, then reduce the heat to medium low and simmer for about 10 more minutes. Add orzo and cook for 10 more minutes or until the orzo is soft. Add the 'chicken'. Adjust the liquid levels if needed. remove from the heat.

 

Whisk together the eggs and lemon juice in a bowl. Whisk in 3 ladles of broth into the egg mixture. Whisk the mixture back into the soup. and cook on low heat for 2 - 3 minutes until thickened. Do not boil. Mix in herbs.

 

Michela doesn't like the eggs in the soup so we usually make it without, tweaking the other ingredients to taste. We thickened it with a little arrow root.

 

Garnish with lemon wedges or peels and some chopped herbs.
