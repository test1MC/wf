Mushroom Stew

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2011

Mushroom broth

Portabela cream soup, one without cream if you can find it

Veggie sausages cut in half lengthwise ten into medaions

one large onion, chopped

Brussels sprouts, Halved or quartered, depending on their size

Carrots, sliced

Sugar snap peas

Crameni mushrooms, sliced

Shitake mushrooms, minced

Fresh thyme, minced, to taste

2 Potatoes, cubed

Kale

 

Heat olive oil in a soup pot. Add onions and sausage when hot. Cook until they start to brown. Add the soups and heat. Add the rest of teh veggies except for the Kale. Add the kale when the other vegetables are just about done.
