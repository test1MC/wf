Moroccan Stew

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2009

1 tbsp olive oil

1 onion, diced

4 cloves of garlic, minced

1 tsp cumin

1 tsp tumeric

1 tsp paprika

1 cinnamon stick, around 4" long

1 sorrento chili, minced

1 - 1¼ sweet potatoes or yams or both, cubed ¾"

1 can garbanzo beans

1 15oz can fire roasted tomatoes, diced

3¾ cups vegetable broth, low sodium

⅛ - ¼ tsp tarragon, to taste - Do not add too much.

 

Garnish:

¼ cup non-fat Greek yogurt

¼ cup cilantro, chopped

lemon or lime wedges

 

heat the oil in a stew pot. Add onion, garlic, peppers, spices, salt and pepper. Cook, stirring often, until onions are translucent.

 

Add potatoes/yams, broth, beans, tomatoes and heat until it starts to boil. Reduce the heat to a simmer and cook until the potatoes are tender. Salt and pepper to taste.

 

Garnish individually to taste.

 

Can be served over rice or quinoa.
