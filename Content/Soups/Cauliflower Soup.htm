Cauliflower Soup

Recipe Courtesy of Frank, 2000

1 Tablespoon oil

4+ cups chopped cauliflower

1+ cup chopped onion

1+ cup chopped celery

2½ cups water

2 teaspoons instant vegetable broth

1 - 2 bay leaves

¼ teaspoon salt

⅛ teaspoon Pepper

⅛ dried basil

⅛ dried tarragon

1 cups of evaporated milk

 

Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add cauliflower, onions and celery. Cook 15 minutes, stirring frequently. Add small amounts of water, to keep vegetables from sticking (if needed).

 

Add 2½ cups of water and the remaining ingredients, except milk. The water level should be just below the top of the vegetables. Bring to a boil, cover, reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 minutes, or until vegetables are very tender. Remove and discard the bay leaves.

 

Place about 2/3 of the vegetables in a blender or food processor. Blend until smooth adding a little of the soup liquid if necessary. Return the blended mixture to the saucepan. Add milk and heat through (Do not boil.)

 

 

 

 

 

Yields about 6 one cup servings
