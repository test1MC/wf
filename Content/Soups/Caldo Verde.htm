Caldo Verde

Recipe courtesy of Michela & Frank, 2007

olive oil

1 large onion (chopped)

3 garlic cloves (minced)

10 oz Boca Italian veggie sausage (halved lengthwise and cut into slices)

5 cups vegetable broth

2 medium baking potatoes (peeled and cubed)

4+ cups kale (stemmed and shredded)

½ tsp salt

½ tsp pepper

New Mexican Green Chili to taste - around 6 (roasted, peeled and chopped with garlic)

 

Heat a large saucepan with oil over medium-high heat. Add onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for about 30 seconds. Add the sausage and cook until it starts to brown, about 5 minutes. Pour in the broth and add potatoes, heat to boil cover and simmer for about 5 minutes, stirring often.

 

Add kale cover and continue to simmer, stirring occasionally, until kale is tender (about 10 minutes). Stir in salt, pepper and chili (to taste). Add more broth as needed.  The liquid level should be about 1' over the top of the solids (a stew like consistency). Let simmer for 5 - 10 more minutes, adjusting the spice level until everything is tender.

 

Add garlic power to taste.

 

Serve with a good multi-grain bread.

 

Makes about 4 main course servings.
