Potato Corn Chowder

Recipe courtesy of Eric Bearden, 2004

8-10 ears of fresh corn, shaved with excess corn milk squeezed from cobs

6 larger peeled and chopped potatoes

1 diced jalapeno

1¼ cups chopped onion

¾ cup chopped green and red peppers

minced garlic to taste

3 cans vegetable broth

1 can cream of mushroom soup

3 cups milk

1 cup half and half

salt and pepper to taste

2 tablespoons of butter

2 cups grated cheddar cheese

 

Saute onion, potatoes, peppers, and minced garlic in butter in hot skillet until potatoes have slightly browned and onions are translucent. Combine these ingredients with the corn, broth, and cream of mushroom in crock pot and cook on LOW for 6-7 hours.

 

1 hour before serving, add the milk and half and half and cheese and increase to HIGH. Can take vegetable ingredients from crock pot and puree before adding milk and cheese for a smoother chowder.

 

 

 

 

 

Yields: about 6 servings
