Strawberries Cardinal

Recipe courtesy of Chambord, 2002

3 cups strawberries

10 oz. frozen raspberries

1/3 cup confectioners sugar

¼ cup Chambord

1 cup sweetened whipped cream

 

 Wash and hull strawberries and divide into four equal parts.  Puree raspberries in a blender with the sugar and Chambord. Strain to remove seeds (optional).  Either pour sauce over strawberries in stemmed glasses and chill for up to six hours, or place a layer of berries in a glass, pour a little sauce over it, then layer whipped cream, repeat until the glass is full.

 

 Serve over brownies with some chocolate syrup drizzled over it.

 

 

 

Yields 4 servings
