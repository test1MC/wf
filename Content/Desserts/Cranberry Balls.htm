Cranberry Balls

Recipe courtesy of Michela, 2003

1 cup pine nuts

1 cup sunflower seeds

1 cup whole almonds

2 cups dried cranberries, or dried fruit of your choice

2 tbsps maple syrup

1 tsp vanilla extract

1 tsp nutmeg

coconut flakes

 

Place the pine nuts, sunflower seeds and almonds in a food processor and pulse until coarsely ground.  Add the dried fruit and pulse 3 or 4 times, then add the maple syrup, vanilla and nutmeg.  Continue to pulse until the mixture begins to stick together.  Taste for sweetness and add more maple syrup if necessary. Place the coconut flakes in a flat bowl. Roll the nut and fruit mixture into one inch balls and then roll in coconut flakes to coat. Store in an airtight container.

 

 

 

 

 

Makes about 20 balls
