Southern Pecan Pie

Recipe courtesy of Shawn Johnson, 2004

1 cup pecan halves

1 9 in pie shell unbaked

3 eggs

1 tbsp butter (melted)

1 cup light corn syrup

½ tsp vanilla (full)

1 cup sugar

1 tbsp flour

(dash of salt – not more than a dash)

 

Place pecans in the bottom of the pie shell. Beat eggs, butter, corn syrup and vanilla. Stir until well blended.

Usually beat the eggs first and then stir in all the remainder.

Stir well until well blended. Combine sugar and flour: Blend with egg mixture.

Pour over nuts and pie shell.

Let stand until the nuts rise onto the surface.

Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes – or until the middle of the pie is firm. (check with knife – needs to come out clean).
