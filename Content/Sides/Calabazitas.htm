Calabazitas

Recipe Courtesy of Michela, 1999

6 roasted New Mexico green chilies

4 tbsp butter

3 cloves of garlic

3 - 4 ears of corn, kernels cut off the cob

2 green zucchinis

1 yellow squash

1 medium sweet onion

Salt and pepper

 

Saute the garlic, onions and 2 tbsp of butter in a skillet until soft. Add the remainder of the butter, corn and zucchinis and cook for 2 minutes. Add the green chili and cook another 5 to 10 minutes. until the squash and corn is done. Salt and pepper to taste

 

Add some browned tofu to the pan about 5 minutes before serving to make it a complete meal.
