Asparagus Sauteed with Pecans

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 1999

1 lb. or so asparagus

⅛ cup chopped pecans

balsamic vinegar (splash)

1 tsp olive oil

salt / pepper to taste

 

- Heat the oil in a skillet.

- Saute the asparagus for about 5 minutes or until just about done.

- Add the chopped pecans cook for about a minute or two longer.

- Add balsamic vinegar, a little at a time to taste.

 

 

 

 

 

Yields 3 to 4 servings
