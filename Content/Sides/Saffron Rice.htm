Saffron Rice

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2001

1 cup jasmine rice

½ cup water

1½ cup vegetable broth

Pinch of saffron

Salt and pepper to taste

 

Place rice water saffron and broth into a sauce pan. Cover and heat until it comes to a boil, reduce to a simmer. cook for 15 minutes or until all the liquid is gone. Let sit off the heat and covered for 10 minutes.

 

 

 

 

 

Yields: 2 servings
