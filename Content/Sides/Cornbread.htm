Granny's Cornbread

Recipe courtesy of Granny

½ cup vegetable shortening or lard

1 large egg

½ cup self rising flour

1 cup cornmeal

1 cup buttermilk

Salt

 

Mix egg, flour, cornmeal and buttermilk together in a bowl. The consistency should be a little thinner than/same as grits.

Pre-heat oven to 450 degrees. Put shortening in cast iron skillet and melt in pre-heated oven, then once melted, pour into mixture. Put mixture back in hot skillet and bake 15 minutes (if electric oven, may take up to 18 minutes). Broil to brown the top and turn out on plate.

Chop up some jalapeno chilies to add to the batter.

Serve with Chock Full of Veggies Chili or Creole Jambalaya.

 

This recipe is from Steph's Granny from Tennessee.
