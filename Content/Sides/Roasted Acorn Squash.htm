Roasted Acorn Squash

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2000

2 Acorn squash, cut in half and seeded

1 tbsp ground ginger (optional)

2 tbsp brown sugar

2 tbsp butter (melted) Salt

 

Preheat oven to 350°.

Cut the acorn squash in half and remove seeds. Mix melted butter, ginger and brown sugar in a small bowl (Taste and adjust the flavor if needed). Evenly drizzle the flesh of the acorn squashes with the mixture allowing it to drip into the area where the seeds were. Sprinkle salt over the squash. Place flesh side up on a baking sheet and bake in the oven for about 40 - 45 minutes, or until tender.

 

To help the squash stay level while cooking and preparing, make small "rings" using aluminum fold and set each squash on one.

 

 

 

Yields 4 servings
