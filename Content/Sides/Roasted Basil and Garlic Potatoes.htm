Roasted Basil and Garlic Potatoes

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2002

4-5 red potatoes (copped into bite sized pieces)

3 cloves garlic (minced)

10+ leaves of fresh basil

2+ tbsp butter

Salt and Pepper

 

Preheat the oven to 400°.

In a pot with salted water boil the potatoes (with or without the skin) until almost done. They should still be a little undercooked. Drain well.

 

Add butter to a large pan and saute the garlic for about 3 - 4 minutes. Add potatoes and tore up basil leaves. Saute for about 4 - 5 minutes. Salt and pepper to taste.

 

Pour into an oven safe bowel and bake uncovered until the top is golden brown.

 

 

 

 

 

Yields: 4 servings
