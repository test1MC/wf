Baked Sweet Potato and Pecans

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2002

4 medium sweet potatoes

olive oil

1½ medium sweet onion (cut into bit-sized pieces)

1-2 tbsps butter

4-5 tsp brown sugar

1 tsp cinnamon

nutmeg (dash)

2 oz whole or crushed pecans

Salt and pepper to taste

 

Preheat oven to 425°

Rub each sweet potato with Olive oil and salt, wrap each in aluminum foil and bake for 35 to 45 minutes, until not quite cooked through. Remove from the oven and peel. Cut the potatoes into bit-sized pieces and add to a skillet. Add butter and onions and cook (adding more butter as needed) until the onions and potatoes are about done. Gently stir in brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg and nuts. Cook until warm and mixed thoroughly. Place in a round casserole dish or glass dish with lid and bake covered for about 10 - 15 minutes.

 

Walnuts can be used in place of pecans.

The spices can be adjusted to your taste before the dish is placed in the oven.

 

 

Yields: 4 servings
