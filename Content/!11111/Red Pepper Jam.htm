Red Pepper Jam

Recipe courtesy of Erin Jackson's Grandma, 2009

8 cups sweet red peppers - ground (6 lbs or 18 peppers)

2 T salt

 

stand overnight and drain

 

Add:

2 cups sugar

1 1/3 cups vinegar

 

boil until thick (45min-1 hour)

seal in hot sterilized jars and parafin

 

Serve over cheese get the type from Erin..
