Deviled Eggs

Recipe courtesy of The Fat Flush Plan, 2002

 

2 large eggs, hard -boiled

1/2 tablespoon flaxseed oil

1/2 tsp apple cider vinegar

1/4 tsp dried mustard

1/2 tsp green onion (white part), finely minced

1/4 to 1/2 tsp finely minced garlic (to taste)

Pinch of cayenne pepper (optional)

Sprigs of fresh dill and parsley, for garnish

 

•Cool hard-boiled eggs in cold water.

•Peel eggs and cut in half lengthwise.

•Remove yolks and place in bowl. Add oil, vinegar, onion, garlic, and mustard to yolks, and mix thoroughly.

•Scoop the yolk mixture with a spoon, and use it to fill the egg halves.

•Top with dill and parsley.

 

Suggestions

 

Yields: 1 to 2 Servings
