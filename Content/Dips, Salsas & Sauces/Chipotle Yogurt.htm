Chipotle Yogurt

Recipe courtesy of Isabel's Cantina, 2007

1/4 cup chipotle in adobo sauce (or to taste)

1 cup non-fat Greek yogurt

 

Blend the two ingredients in a food processor until smooth

 

A great dip or topping for french fries, Dragon Potatoes & Yams (see page 1), steak, pork or as a spread for sandwiches.

 

Yields: 1 cup
