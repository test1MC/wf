Salsa Fresca

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2006

2+ vine ripened tomatoes (chopped)

1 tbsp cilantro (chopped)

red onion (chopped)

1 jalapeno or hot chili (minced)

salt and pepper to taste

 

Mix all ingredients into a bowl. Adjust quantities to taste. Salt and pepper to taste.

 

Add lemon or lime juice to taste for added flavor.

 

Use as a topping for dishes or just a salsa for chips.
