Black Bean and Mango Salsa

Recipe courtesy of Michela, 2001

1 ripe mango - peeled and diced

¼ red bell pepper - diced

¼ green bell bepper - diced

¼ red onion - diced

½ cup black beans (canned)

⅓ cup pineapple juice

Juice of 2 limes

¼ cup chopped cilantro

1 tbsp ground cumin

½ tbsp minced green chili

salt and pepper to taste

 

Combine the mango. peppers, onion, black beans, juices, cilantro, cumin and chili pepper in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper.  

 

 

 

 

 

 Can be chilled and covered, for up to 5 days.

Yields 4 to 6 servings
