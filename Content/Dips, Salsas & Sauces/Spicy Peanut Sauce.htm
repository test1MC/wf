Spicy Peanut Sauce

Recipe courtesy of Gourmet , 1995

3 garlic cloves, minced

¼ tsp dried hot red pepper flakes, or to taste

1 tbsp vegetable oil

1 tbsp tomato paste

3 tbsp creamy peanut butter

3 tbsp hoisin sauce

1/2 tsp sugar

3/4 cup water

 

In a small saucepan cook garlic and red pepper flakes in oil over moderate heat, stirring, until garlic is golden. Whisk in remaining ingredients and bring to a boil, whisking. Simmer sauce, whisking, until thickened, about 1 minute. Sauce may be made 3 days ahead and chilled, covered. Serve sauce warm or at room temperature.

 

Use as accompaniment to Herb Spring Rolls (see page 1).

 

Yields: about 1 cup
