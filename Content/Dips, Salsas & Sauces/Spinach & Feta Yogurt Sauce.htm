Spinach & Feta Yogurt Sauce

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 1998

1 cup plain yogurt

5 oz frozen spinach (boxed)

2 oz feta cheese (crumbled)

Juice from ½ lemon

1 garlic clove

salt and pepper to taste

 

Combine ingredients in a food processor and blend. Season to taste.

 

This is a good accompaniment to Stuffed Grape Leaves (page 1).
