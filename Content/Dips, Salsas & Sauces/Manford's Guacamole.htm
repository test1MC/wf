Manford's Guacamole

Recipe courtesy of Manford Griego, 1972

4 large ripe avocados

1 sweet onion, chopped

5 - 6 New Mexico Green Chilies, roasted and peeled

1 - 2 cloves garlic

1 - 2 tbsp sour cream

2 medium vine ripened tomatoes, chopped

Juice from ¼ of lemon

Dash of Worcestershire Sauce (vegetarian)

Salt and pepper

 

Place avocados in a bowel and mash with fork. Finely chop chilies and garlic together. Add the chilies and the rest of the ingredients to the avocado and mix well. Salt and pepper to taste.

 

Leave one or two avocado seeds in the guacamole to prevent browning.

Serve with tortilla chips.

 

 

Yields: 4 servings

Preparation Time: 5 minutes
